Welcome to the CCJam website!

CCJam is an event made almost a year ago to bring ComputerCraft programmers together to share what wonderful programs they could make in a limited amount of time. Although the project was not such a success last year, we at GoldProgramming (the hosters of the event) are hoping that it will turn out better this year.

CCJam is an event that was made around April of 2013; it never really took off. Only a few people attended the event, and fewer could stream their code. Hopefully, this year will be more successful, with participation from a much larger span of people.

Entry is simple - to your right, you will see one or more panels. On one of them is a button, that says [register]. You can click that button and you are transported to an IRC chatroom, built specifically for CCJam. This feature is hosted by the EsperNet IRC servers, and we wish that you would respect their rules.

Registration panel

There are two ways of registering. You may either send us an e-mail on the ComputerCraft forums, or, join us in our IRC channel.
